How to Use EverNet Music

Simple steps to add music to your YouTube Shorts

1

Copy Audio Code

Copy the unique code from your preferred track in the music library.

2

Open YouTube App

Launch YouTube app on your mobile device and create a new Short.

3

Select Video Content

Choose your video content and tap "Add Sound" for music.

4

Paste Audio Code

Paste the copied code in YouTube search to find the track.

5

Confirm Selection

Tap the check button to confirm music selection for your Short.

6

Adjust Volume Settings

Set volume to at least 15% for proper monetization tracking.
