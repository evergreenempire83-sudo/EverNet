How to Use EverNet Music

Simple steps to add music to your YouTube Shorts

1

Copy Audio Code

Copy the unique code from your preferred track.

2

Open YouTube

Launch YouTube app and create a new Short.

3

Select Video

Choose video and tap "Add Sound" for music.

4

Paste Code

Paste code in YouTube search to find track.

5

Confirm Selection

Tap check button to confirm music selection.

6

Adjust Volume

Set volume to at least 15% for monetization.

7

Upload Video

Add details and publish to start earning.

Content Restrictions

(Not Eligible for Revenue)

Music-based Content

Do not use on cover songs, instrumental performances, or live stage footage.

If video has existing music, do not add EverNet tracks.

  • Revenue may not be recorded
  • Videos may be restricted
  • Excluded from settlement

Other Restrictions

  • Low-effort or meaningless content
  • Extremely short repeated uploads
  • Violations of YouTube policies

Approved Music Only

Use only music listed on official EverNet website.

Library updated regularly with licensed tracks.

Unlisted tracks may not count for revenue.

Follow guidelines for stable earnings.
